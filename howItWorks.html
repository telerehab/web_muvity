How it works

Physiotherapists work together with patients to develop the best program custom-made for them.

Community

Program aimed at people in a vulnerable position and accesible to everyone.

Rehab

Gamified telerehabilitation system aimed at post-stroke patients

Customizable

You are able to customize the treatment and the system adjusts to each and every user.

Clinical relevant data

Captures 3D movement, dynamic analysis and AI used to improve physiotherapy.

The app is developed using Unity3D. Programmers work together with physiotherapists to create the best activities for patients so thay can work from their own home everyday, which makes it more accessible for everyone, you just need internet connection, so that your therapist can monitor your progress.
