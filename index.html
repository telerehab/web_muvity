About Muvity

Muvity is a telerehabilitation application that enables the active participation of the user in his own rehabilitation, and is a tool for therapists to monitor the evolution of their patients

Rehab

Gamified telerehabilitation system aimed at subjects with neuromuscular disorders

Customizable

The therapist adapts the treatment according to your evolution remotely

Clinical relevant data

3D capture, kinematics and dynamics analyses to improve physiotherapy outcomes

Flexible

Muvity adapats the goals of the exergames according to the capabilities of the user
